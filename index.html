Services

Exterior Detail ▼
An exterior detail begins with a gentle hand wash that safely removes dirt and road buildup while keeping the paint in great condition. After the wash, the tires are cleaned and refreshed, the exterior glass is polished for clear visibility, and the vehicle is carefully dried to prevent streaks or water spots. The service finishes with a full walk‑around inspection to make sure the exterior looks clean, even, and well‑presented from every angle.
Interior Detail ▼
An interior detail starts with a deep vacuum of the seats, carpets, mats, and trunk area to remove dirt and debris from both open and hard‑to‑reach spots. Carpets and upholstery are then shampooed and treated to lift stains and refresh the fabric, helping the interior look and feel noticeably cleaner. All plastic, vinyl, and leather surfaces are gently cleaned and conditioned to restore a smooth, natural finish, while vents and tight areas are brushed and air‑blown to clear out hidden dust. The windows are polished to a streak‑free shine, and the service wraps up with an odor neutralizer to leave the cabin smelling clean and fresh.
Full Detail ▼
A full detail brings together both the interior and exterior services to give the entire vehicle a refreshed, well‑cared‑for look. The exterior receives a gentle hand wash to remove dirt and buildup while keeping the paint safe, and the vehicle is carefully dried to prevent streaks or water spots. Inside, the cabin is renewed through deep vacuuming, stain‑lifting shampoo work, and the use of steam to clean and sanitize surfaces more thoroughly. All interior materials are cleaned and conditioned, the windows are polished for a clear finish, and the service wraps up with a full inspection to ensure the vehicle looks clean, balanced, and refreshed from top to bottom.
Refresh Package ▼
The refresh package is the most in‑depth service available, designed to bring a vehicle as close to like‑new condition as possible. The interior receives a full restoration that includes deep extraction, stain removal, steam cleaning, and detailed attention to every surface, with the seats removed to allow for an even more thorough clean underneath and around hard‑to‑reach areas. The exterior is carefully washed, decontaminated, and polished for a clean, even finish, and the entire vehicle is inspected from top to bottom to ensure a consistent, refreshed look. This package includes every add‑on option by default and often takes the full day to complete due to the level of detail involved.

Add‑ons

Tire shine enhances the look of your tires by giving them a clean, dark, refreshed finish.
Seat removal allows for a much deeper interior clean by giving access to areas that normally can’t be reached. With the seats taken out, dirt, debris, and stains hidden underneath can be fully cleaned, leaving the interior noticeably fresher and more complete.
Headlight restoration removes the cloudy, yellowed layer that builds up over time and brings back a clear, bright finish. This improves both the look of the vehicle and nighttime visibility.
Engine bay cleaning removes dust, grease, and buildup from the engine area using safe cleaning methods. The result is a cleaner, more presentable engine compartment that looks well‑maintained.
Full body polishing helps reduce the look of light scratches, swirl marks, and dullness in the paint. It brings back a smoother, glossier finish and makes the vehicle’s exterior look brighter and more refined.
